Business funding content audit

Business funding pages usually break when the promise gets ahead of the process.

Use this audit angle for landing pages, funding explainers, broker pages, RBF or MCA copy, and any page that needs clearer eligibility language before it gets reviewed or summarized.

Run the audit Request manual audit

Direct answer

A business funding content audit checks landing page clarity, process description, rate and speed claims, product positioning, and whether the page uses eligibility-based wording instead of promises.

Landing pages

Check heroes, trust notes, and CTA language first.

Funding explainers

Make the process and tradeoffs clearer than the hype.

Broker pages

Reduce overreach around approval, speed, and fit.

RBF or MCA pages

Clarify mechanics, timing, and limitations without pretending the product is universal.

What it does not guarantee

No guaranteed funding, approvals, rates, or outcomes. The point is to make the page clearer and easier to review before publish.

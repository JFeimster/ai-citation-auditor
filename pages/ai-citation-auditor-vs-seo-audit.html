Comparison

AI Citation Auditor and a standard SEO audit solve different problems on the same finance page.

A traditional SEO audit checks rankings, crawl signals, indexability, metadata, and competitive gaps. AI Citation Auditor focuses on answer extraction, claim safety, trust signals, schema fit, and whether the page is easy for answer engines to summarize without distortion.

Run the audit Request manual review

Direct answer

Use a standard SEO audit when visibility and technical performance are the main issue. Use AI Citation Auditor when the content itself needs a better answer shape, clearer boundaries, and safer finance wording.

Scope

One review checks search mechanics. The other checks whether the page can survive AI summarization.

Choose SEO audit first when

The page has crawl, duplication, metadata, ranking, or site-structure issues that are bigger than the copy itself.

Choose AI Citation Auditor first when

The page is already live or nearly publish-ready, but the answer is weak, the wording is risky, or the structure is hard to extract.

Use both when

You need technical cleanup and a tighter editorial review path for finance-specific claims and answer blocks.

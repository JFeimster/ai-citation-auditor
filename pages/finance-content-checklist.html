FAQ

Questions before you publish.

Does clearing this checklist guarantee AI citations or rankings?

No. It improves how clear, trustworthy, and extractable your content is — the part you control. No checklist or tool can guarantee rankings, AI citations, traffic, approvals, or financial outcomes.

Is my progress saved if I leave the page?

No. The checkboxes run entirely in your browser for the session and reset on reload. Use Copy or Download to keep a version, or print it for an editing pass.

What’s the difference between “Must fix” and “Should fix”?

Must fix items block publishing — usually answer clarity and risky-claim wording. Should fix items strengthen the page soon after, and Nice to have items are upgrades when you have time.

Is this only for finance content?

It’s strongest for finance, fintech, funding, lending, business credit, and ecommerce finance content where clarity and claim safety matter most. The structure items apply broadly; the wording guardrails are finance-specific.

Can I hand this to a writer or editor?

Yes — that’s the point. Copy the markdown into a brief, download the .md, or print it so a writer, editor, SEO, or compliance reviewer can work the list before anything goes live.

Comparison

General ChatGPT is flexible. AI Citation Auditor is narrower, more opinionated, and better suited to finance-content review.

General ChatGPT is useful for drafting, ideation, and generic rewrite help. AI Citation Auditor is built to review finance pages for answer extraction, risky claims, trust signals, schema fit, internal links, and page-level readiness before publishing.

Boundary setting

An AI search audit can flag clarity and claim-risk issues, but it is not a compliance approval process.

AI Citation Auditor reviews whether finance content is easy to summarize, easy to extract, and less likely to overstate the offer. That is useful editorial screening. It does not replace legal review, compliance review, underwriting rules, or internal approval workflows.

Direct answer

Use the AI search audit to catch obvious editorial risk and structure problems earlier. Use compliance review for approval, policy interpretation, and formal sign-off.

What the audit is good at

Spotting vague answers, weak H2s, poor comparison logic, missing disclaimers, and risky claim phrasing before the review queue gets the draft.

What it is not good at

Giving policy approval, legal interpretation, or any claim that the content is compliant, approved, or safe to publish without review.

Safer workflow

Use the audit to clean the draft, then route the revised page through the real review process before publishing.

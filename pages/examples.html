Example 2

Rewrite target

“Guaranteed approval, fast funding, and the best terms for every business.”

Safer direction: explain what the offer is, who it may fit, and what criteria still matter.

Risky phraseSafer phrase
Guaranteed approvalFit-based evaluation
Fast fundingFaster access where eligible
Best termsPotentially competitive terms

Before / after

Short paragraphs beat inflated copy when the reader needs a decision.

Before

We deliver the fastest, smartest, and most reliable funding path for every business.

After

We help business owners compare funding options, understand eligibility, and decide whether the offer fits their situation.

Why it works

It says what the page does without promising a result the page cannot control.

Where to use it

Landing pages, comparison pages, FAQ sections, and hero copy.

FAQ

Do these examples represent guarantees?

Are these meant to be copied word-for-word?

No. They are patterns. The point is to show the level of specificity and caution the audit should produce.

Can the GPT create a table like this from my page?

Yes. Tables are one of the most useful parts of the audit because they make the revision target obvious.

What is the safest next step after the audit?

Apply the rewrite, add missing structure, and review the page with your normal business and compliance process.

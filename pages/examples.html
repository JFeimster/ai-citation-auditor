Risky phrase Safer rewrite

Missing FAQ block

Add a visible FAQ when the page needs to answer objections or process questions.

  • What does this page help with?
  • Who is this for?
  • What does it not guarantee?
  • What should I review before publishing?

Table recommendation

Use a comparison table when the page needs a faster decision path.

A useful table should compare options, criteria, or revision status rather than repeat the same paragraph in columns.

Good columns: option, criteria, fit, next step.

Starter prompt

“Show me the highest-risk claims on this finance page, then rewrite them into safer language and give me a checklist of what to fix before publish.”

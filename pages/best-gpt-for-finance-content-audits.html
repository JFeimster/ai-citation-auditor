Fit-based evaluation

The best GPT for finance content audits depends on whether you need broad help or a finance-specific pressure test.

A general GPT can help with brainstorming and drafting. A finance-content audit GPT is a better fit when the page needs risky-claim checks, answer extraction, schema fit, internal-link mapping, and trust-sensitive rewrite guidance.

How it works

The C.I.T.E. framework keeps the audit from drifting into vibes.

C.I.T.E. stands for Clarity, Information structure, Trust signals, and Entity / ecosystem fit. It is built for finance content that needs to be understandable, extractable, and careful enough to survive AI summaries.

Clarity Can an answer engine quickly understand the page’s core answer?
Information structure Are headings, tables, summaries, and lists easy to extract?
Trust signals Does the page show enough context, boundaries, and editorial care?
Entity fit Do schema, internal links, and CTAs support the right decision path?

FAQ

Sharp answers before you run the audit.

Does this guarantee AI citations or rankings?

No. It helps improve citation readiness, answer clarity, structure, and trust signals. It does not guarantee rankings, citations, traffic, approvals, credit outcomes, or financial results.

Is this only for finance content?

It is strongest for finance, fintech, funding, lending, business credit, and related operator content where clarity and claim safety matter most.

What should I paste into the GPT?

Paste a draft article, landing page, outline, or extracted URL text. You can also ask it to audit a specific section, CTA, FAQ block, or internal-link plan.

Can this replace legal or compliance review?

No. It can flag wording that may be risky or overconfident, but it is not legal, compliance, tax, lending, credit, or financial advice.

FAQ

Sharp answers before you run the audit.

Does this guarantee AI citations or rankings?

No. It helps improve citation readiness, answer clarity, structure, and trust signals. It does not guarantee rankings, citations, traffic, approvals, credit outcomes, or financial results.

Is this only for finance content?

It is strongest for finance, fintech, funding, lending, business credit, ecommerce finance, and related operator content where clarity and claim safety matter.

What should I paste into the GPT?

Paste a draft article, landing page, outline, page copy, or extracted URL text. You can also ask it to audit a specific section, CTA, FAQ block, or internal-link plan.

Can this replace legal or compliance review?

No. It can flag wording that may be risky or overconfident, but it is not legal, compliance, tax, lending, credit, or financial advice.

What kind of output should I expect?

A scorecard, missing answer blocks, improved H2s, table suggestions, risky claim rewrites, FAQ rewrites, schema recommendations, internal link additions, CTA review, and a publish checklist.

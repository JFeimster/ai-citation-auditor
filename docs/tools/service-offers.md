# Service Offers

## Single-Page AI Citation Audit
- Who it is for: teams with one important finance page to review.
- Problem it solves: unclear answer blocks, risky claims, weak structure, and missing trust signals.
- Deliverables: scorecard, rewrite notes, H2 ideas, FAQ ideas, schema notes, and internal link suggestions.
- Inputs required: page URL or page copy.
- Suggested CTA: Request a manual audit.
- Risk-safe positioning: review-before-publishing support.
- What it does not promise: rankings, citations, approvals, funding, or traffic.

## Five-Page Finance Content Refresh
- Who it is for: teams with a small cluster of related pages.
- Problem it solves: repeated copy issues across a group of pages.
- Deliverables: page-by-page audit notes, rewrite guidance, and cluster-level recommendations.
- Inputs required: five page URLs or page copies.
- Suggested CTA: Ask for availability.
- Risk-safe positioning: clearer, safer, more structured content.
- What it does not promise: guaranteed search or AI visibility.

## Finance Content Risk Rewrite Package
- Who it is for: teams with risky funding, credit, lending, or fintech copy.
- Problem it solves: overconfident claims and compliance-sensitive wording.
- Deliverables: safer rewrite options, claim bank updates, and a review checklist.
- Inputs required: page copy and the business goal for each page.
- Suggested CTA: Request a rewrite brief.
- Risk-safe positioning: wording cleanup and clarity support.
- What it does not promise: compliance approval or legal advice.

## Internal Link Map Sprint
- Who it is for: teams with a hub-and-spoke content set.
- Problem it solves: orphan pages and weak topical connections.
- Deliverables: hub map, spoke map, anchor text ideas, and link placement notes.
- Inputs required: URL list or sitemap.
- Suggested CTA: Get an internal link audit.
- Risk-safe positioning: structure and discoverability support.
- What it does not promise: ranking guarantees.

## AI-Search Optimization Sprint
- Who it is for: finance teams preparing pages for AI answer engines.
- Problem it solves: pages that are hard to extract, trust, or summarize.
- Deliverables: answer block guidance, FAQ ideas, schema fit notes, and cleaner section structure.
- Inputs required: target pages and business priorities.
- Suggested CTA: Open the GPT or request a manual audit.
- Risk-safe positioning: citation readiness and extraction support.
- What it does not promise: guaranteed citations or AI placement.

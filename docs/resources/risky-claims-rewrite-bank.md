# Risky Claims Rewrite Bank

## Funding

| Risky phrase | Why it is risky | Safer rewrite |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Get funded fast | Implies speed and outcome certainty | Explore funding options faster based on eligibility |
| Guaranteed approval | Promises a decision that cannot be guaranteed | See whether you may qualify based on current criteria |
| Instant funding | Suggests a timeline guarantee | Review funding timelines and eligibility requirements |
| Approved today | Overstates certainty and timing | Check available next steps and review requirements |

## Credit

| Risky phrase | Why it is risky | Safer rewrite |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Improve your credit score | Implies a specific result | Learn factors that may affect your credit profile |
| Fix your credit fast | Suggests rapid outcome certainty | Review practical steps that may help you organize next actions |
| Boost your score overnight | Unrealistic and misleading | See what changes may influence your score over time |
| Credit repair guaranteed | Overpromises a regulated outcome | Review available credit education and support options |

## Lending

| Risky phrase | Why it is risky | Safer rewrite |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Best loan for everyone | Overgeneralizes a fit-based decision | Compare loan options based on business needs and criteria |
| Lowest rate guaranteed | Rate promises are unstable and often unsupported | Review current rate ranges and eligibility-based pricing |
| No-risk loan | Lending always has tradeoffs | Understand the terms, fees, and decision factors first |
| Fast approval with no docs | Suggests an unrealistic approval path | Check documentation requirements before you apply |

## Fintech

| Risky phrase | Why it is risky | Safer rewrite |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Works for every business | Too broad to be credible | May fit businesses that need the listed use case |
| Seamless onboarding | Hides real implementation effort | Streamlined onboarding with clear setup steps |
| One-size-fits-all solution | Often false in finance products | Fit-based solution for the stated audience |
| Automates compliance | Could imply regulated approval | Helps structure internal review workflows |

## SEO / AI Citation Claims

| Risky phrase | Why it is risky | Safer rewrite |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Guaranteed citations | No one can guarantee AI citations | May improve citation readiness and extractability |
| Guaranteed rankings | Search rankings change and cannot be promised | Can help structure pages for better search visibility |
| Guaranteed traffic | Traffic depends on many external factors | May support clearer content and discoverability |
| AI will cite your page | AI systems choose sources independently | This can make your page easier for AI to understand and summarize |

## Notes by Category

### Funding
- Use eligibility-based language.
- Mention timelines only when they are clearly stated and current.
- Avoid outcome language that implies certainty.

### Credit
- Keep the copy educational.
- Avoid claims that imply score control or instant improvement.
- Use review and learning language instead of promise language.

### Lending
- Separate product facts from outcome language.
- Do not imply approval, rate, or underwriting certainty.
- Add review-before-publishing notes when the page mentions terms or criteria.

### Fintech
- Keep product claims bounded and specific.
- Replace broad superlatives with use-case language.
- Be careful with wording around compliance, automation, and risk.

### SEO / AI Citation Claims
- Use “citation readiness” instead of “guaranteed citations.”
- Use “may improve clarity” instead of “will rank.”
- Keep the claim focused on structure, extraction, and trust signals.

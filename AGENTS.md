# AGENTS.md

## Project
AI Citation Auditor landing page and microsite.

## Goal
Promote a free GPT that audits finance content for AI citation readiness, answer clarity, extraction structure, schema fit, internal links, trust signals, and risky claims.

## Stack
Plain HTML, CSS, and vanilla JavaScript. No build step. No heavy dependencies.

## Brand voice
Premium dark SaaS. Sharp finance-content strategist. Tactical, anti-generic SEO sludge, direct, operator-focused.

## Claim guardrails
Do not claim guaranteed AI citations, rankings, approvals, credit improvement, funding, lending outcomes, compliance approval, or financial outcomes.

## Code rules
- Keep files static and lightweight.
- Use semantic HTML.
- Preserve accessibility labels and focus states.
- Keep CTA links easy to replace.
- Do not add tracking scripts unless explicitly requested.
- Do not invent testimonials, logos, case studies, stats, or partner claims.

## Safe positioning
Use phrases like:
- “AI citation readiness”
- “answer-engine-friendly structure”
- “finance content audit”
- “risk-aware wording”
- “publish checklist”

Avoid phrases like:
- “guaranteed citation”
- “rank #1 in AI”
- “get approved”
- “improve your credit”
- “compliance-approved”
